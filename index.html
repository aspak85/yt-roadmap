Long Video Guide

Idea se upload tak — step-by-step 2026 ka proven formula

📋 7-Step Viral Formula
1

Niche aur Topic Research

VidIQ ya TubeBuddy se trending topics dhundho. 3–5 competitor channels analyze karo. 20 ideas pehle se list karo.

2

Powerful Hook (0–30 sec)

Problem → Promise formula use karo. "Yeh mistake mat karna..." ya "Main aapko aaj bata dunga..." — viewers rukte hain.

3

Script Structure Banao

Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA — 5-part formula. Har 3–4 min pe new point. B-roll plan karo pehle se.

4

Shooting Setup

Window ke samne natural light. Boya ya RODE mic use karo. 1080p minimum, 4K preferred. Clean background zaroori.

5

Editing Tips

Jump cuts har 3–4 sec. Text overlays add karo. CapCut, DaVinci ya Premiere use karo. Auto captions lagao — 40% boost.

6

Viral Thumbnail Banao

Face + Emotion + Text = winning combo. 3 fonts max, 3 colors max. Mobile pe test karo. Studio se A/B test karo.

7

Upload + Optimize

Chapters add karo. Description pehli 2 lines mein keywords. End screens aur cards. Community post ke saath launch.

⏱️ Best Video Length 2026

8–12 min

Tutorial aur tips videos — best AVD percentage milti hai consistently

↕

15–20 min

In-depth guides — higher watch time = more AdSense revenue guaranteed

↕

30–60 min

Documentary style — super fans ke liye. Channel membership badhta hai.

Complete Setup Guide

Zero se professional YouTube channel — sab kuch yahan step by step

📱Channel Banana aur Optimize Karna▼
  • Google account se YouTube.com → profile icon → "Create a channel" — 2 minute ka kaam hai.
  • Channel name: niche + personality ya brand name. Badalne mein time lagta hai — soch ke rakho.
  • Channel art: 2560×1440px banner — Canva free template use karo. Mobile aur TV pe check karo.
  • Profile picture: 800×800px, clear face ya logo. Round crop hogi isliye center mein rakho face.
  • Description: pehli 2 lines mein keywords — yeh Google search mein bhi dikhti hain.
  • Custom URL: 100 subscribers ke baad milta hai @YourName format. Pehle se plan karo name.
🎙️Camera, Mic aur Lighting Setup▼
  • Phone Camera: iPhone 13+ ya Samsung S21+ — aaj ke liye bilkul kafi hai. Gimbal lo (₹1500–3000).
  • Budget Mic (₹1000–2000): Boya BY-M1 Lapel — Amazon pe milti hai. Audio = 70% viewer experience.
  • Mid Range (₹3000–6000): RODE VideoMicro ya Blue Snowball USB mic for voiceover recording.
  • Lighting: Window ki natural light best. Ring light ₹1200–2500. Softbox set ₹4000+ for pro look.
  • Free Editing: CapCut (mobile/PC), DaVinci Resolve (PC) — dono professional quality dete hain.
  • Thumbnail: Canva free plan kafi hai start mein. Photoshop better aur faster results deta hai.
📊YouTube Studio — Sab Kuch Samjho▼
  • Dashboard: Recent performance, subscriber count, news — yahan se start karo apna din har roz.
  • Analytics: Reach → Engagement → Audience — teen tabs most important hain. Weekly check karo.
  • CTR: 4% average, 6%+ good, 10%+ excellent. CTR low hai toh thumbnail turant badlo.
  • AVD: Agar 40%+ hai toh acha hai. Drop kahan hota hai — Analytics mein precisely dekh sakte ho.
  • Traffic Sources: Suggested, Search, External — kahan se views aa rahe hain track karo weekly.
  • A/B Thumbnail Test: Experimental tab mein — do thumbnails test karo, winner automatically set hota hai.
🔧Best Creator Tools 2026▼
  • VidIQ (Free+Paid): Keyword research, competitor analysis, daily ideas — must-have browser extension.
  • TubeBuddy: Tag suggestions, A/B testing, bulk editing. Free plan kafi hai beginners ke liye.
  • Google Trends: Trending topics ka free real-time data. Seasonality samajhne ke liye best.
  • Notion/Trello: Content calendar — 30 video ideas pehle se plan karo systematic tarike se.
  • Canva Pro: Thumbnails, channel art, community post graphics — sab ek jagah professionally.
  • Social Blade: Competitor channels ka data — growth rate, estimated earnings sab dikhta hai free mein.
📅Content Calendar aur Consistency▼
  • Month 1: Ek niche pe focus karo — 8–12 videos. Quality over quantity. Formats experiment karo.
  • Month 2–3: Weekly 2x upload target. Long + Shorts mix. Community posts har 3 din mein.
  • Batch recording: Ek din mein 3–4 videos shoot karo — weekly pressure nahi rahega phir.
  • Content pillars: 3 types — Educational 60%, Entertainment 30%, Promotional 10% ratio rakho.
  • Seasonal planning: IPL, Festivals, Board exams — trending time pe pehle se videos ready rakho.
  • Re-purpose: Long video se 3–5 Shorts nikalo. Audio se podcast. Thumbnail se blog post banao.

🧠 CTR Scoring Algorithm
Yeh tool in 8 factors ke basis pe score karta hai:

1. Curiosity Gap (25 pts) — Kya title mein suspense hai?
2. Number Power (15 pts) — Numbers in title = trust
3. Emotional Trigger (20 pts) — Emotions click badhate hain
4. Keyword Relevance (15 pts) — Search mein milna zaroori
5. Length Score (10 pts) — 50-60 chars ideal hai
6. Power Words (10 pts) — Viral, Secret, Best, Never etc
7. Year/Trend (3 pts) — 2026 add karna +points
8. Question Hook (2 pts) — Question titles perform better
